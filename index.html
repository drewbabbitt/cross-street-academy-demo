Why Cross Street Academy?

What makes us different from other online schools?

Live online private school

Live classes taught every school day, so students learn and interact with their teachers just like a traditional school — from the comfort and control of their own home. As far as we know, we are the only online school that offers this.

Our teachers

Because they teach every day, our teachers are full time — not part-time educators on call. We pay competitively with private schools and attract some of the best teachers in the country as a result. See their bios.

Affordably priced

Our tuition sits at the lowest end of the pricing spectrum. At $2,900/yr, it is a fraction of the equivalent private school cost. See pricing.

Clubs & special events

Four after-school clubs — ESports, Drama, Cooking and Digital Art — host tournaments, create the digital yearbook, and put on an annual talent show and virtual play. Virtual assemblies feature guest speakers, often current or former professional athletes.

The full package

Whether you are looking for a private or public school alternative or augmenting your homeschool, Cross Street has something for everyone: Bible-based classes taught by expert teachers, live classes with on-demand recordings, and an award-winning online curriculum — all for one low price.

Our Mission

A quality Christian education that is accessible and affordable

CSA pursues this by providing a college preparatory education, taught from a biblical worldview, by highly accomplished educators. We partner with parents to train students to be independent thinkers, who seek truth, grow as leaders, and impact their community and culture for Christ.
