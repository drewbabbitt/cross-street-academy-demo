About Cross Street Academy

Our story

From a Facebook community more than 20,000 strong to a live, accredited online Christian school — the mission behind Cross Street Academy, told in our founder’s own words.

A letter from the founder

“Start children off on the way they should go, and even when they are old they will not turn from it.”

Over the past 20 years, I have enjoyed a successful career on Wall Street but always felt compelled to a different path. So, five years ago I started a Facebook page called Cross Street Daily. Ever since, Bible verses and encouraging quotes about God’s grace have been posted and that community has grown to over 20,000. But I still felt I should be doing more.

I have always had a passion for teaching, and coupling that with what I see as a serious need for improved educational opportunities led to the launch of the Cross Street Academy. Educating our children is one of the most important things we do in life. Our goal at Cross Street is to offer the best quality teaching possible with a Christian worldview to help equip our children to excel in this challenging world.

Scott Saiers · Founder, Cross Street Academy
