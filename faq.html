FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who are the teachers?

All of our teachers are highly qualified with a bachelor's degree or higher from an accredited college or university. See their bios.

Do all of the teachers teach with a Christian worldview?

Yes — all of our teachers are followers of Christ and teach each subject from a Christian worldview. The accompanying online program is an award-winning Bible-based curriculum called Ignitia, created by Alpha Omega Publications. See Curriculum.

Does this substitute out the need for parent involvement?

No. Whether you use our academy to enhance your homeschool or as a public or private school alternative, parents need to be involved. The student's family is at the center of online schooling — our academy is a partner in the education process, not a replacement for parental involvement. It is the parent's responsibility to follow their state and local homeschooling laws, as each state has its own requirements.

Do the students get to interact live with their teacher?

Yes. Students can interact with the teacher and other students during the live classes. We use Zoom meetings for our live classes.

Do I have to watch the classes live?

For middle school classes, students are highly encouraged to join live but do not have to — all classes are recorded and available on demand. For high school classes, students do have to join the live classes and attendance is taken.

How does grading work?

All assignments, quizzes and tests are graded by us, with results visible in Ignitia in real time. The overall course grade combines the three, with weightings varying slightly by subject. Due dates and upcoming quiz and test dates are also visible online.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. If you are not satisfied, you can cancel up to 14 days after the school year starts for a full refund (minus an administrative fee).

What technology do I need?

A PC or laptop with reliable internet access for the live classes, plus a webcam so students can actively participate. The online curriculum runs in common internet browsers.

Do you offer dual enrollment?

Yes. We have partnered with the College at Southeastern so students can earn both high school credits and college credits in concurrent classes taught by our teachers.

What grade levels are you offering?

We offer grades 6–12.

Is your program NCAA approved?

Yes, our non-traditional program has been approved by NCAA. See the list of eligible courses.

Is Cross Street Academy accredited?

Cross Street Academy is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC). CSA is also a member of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI), and we use an award-winning curriculum well regarded in the homeschooling Christian community.

What time and when do the classes start?

Classes run from August 21st through May 22nd with breaks for standard school holidays. Classes start at 10:15am ET and run approximately 40 minutes with scheduled breaks in between.

What is your Statement of Faith?
We are Bible based, non-denominational:
  1. We believe in one God who eternally exists in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  2. We believe the Bible to be the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God.
  3. We believe that man was created in God's image, but is separated from God by sin.
  4. We believe in the incarnation and virgin birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, that He is the only begotten Son of God.
  5. We believe Jesus died on the cross to pay for our sins, was buried, and rose from the dead.
  6. We believe that we are redeemed by grace through faith in Jesus's atoning death on the cross and that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life.
  7. We believe salvation is not by good works but is by the grace of God through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  8. We believe Jesus ascended into heaven and will return again.
  9. We believe that the gift of eternal life is available to all, that those who receive Christ by faith are regenerated by the Holy Spirit and thereby become children of God.
