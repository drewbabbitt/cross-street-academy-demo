Curriculum

An award-winning, Bible-based curriculum

Cross Street Academy uses the Ignitia online curriculum, created by Alpha Omega Publications (AOP).

History & Geography

A comprehensive look at history and geography from a biblical perspective — geography, history, citizenship, economics, and social studies, plus special topics like career preparation and finance management.

Levels: 6th, 7th, 8th, World History, American History, Government & Econ

Science

Bible-based science courses explain God’s relationship with the physical universe while developing observation skills — biology, chemistry, life science, space, physical science, and nature.

Levels: 6th, 7th, 8th, Biology, Chemistry, Physics

Language Arts

Literacy in all areas of communication — reading, writing, listening, and speaking — through interactive lessons infused with God’s Word. Upper grades add literature studies, the Bible as literature, research and reporting skills, and visual media.

Levels: 6th–12th grade

Math

From the basics of arithmetic to complex problem solving: online lessons and practice, learning games, challenging assessments, and creative problem solving that applies math to real life.

Levels: 6th, 7th, 8th, Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2, Pre-Calculus

Bible

Inspirational and practical — from basic theology and the attributes of God to biblical literature and geography, students explore the Old and New Testaments through interactive materials.

Levels: 6th, 7th, 8th, Old Testament, New Testament

Spanish

Spanish I and II utilize Rosetta Stone to explore the language through speaking, listening, and interacting. Dual enrollment Spanish is also offered.

High school foreign language credit
