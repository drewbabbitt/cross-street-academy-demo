CSA guidance counseling

Available to help students with their educational, vocational, and social/emotional development — and for high school students, a resource for class planning, college preparation and career planning.

Meet the guidance counselor

Mrs. Mitchell received her master’s degree in clinical mental health counseling and bachelor’s degree in psychology from Truett McConnell University. She has been working as a counselor in Christian private practice with children, teens, adults and couples. Her goal is to help each student navigate struggles, pressures and changes in their life through a biblical perspective.
