NCAA approved courses

Cross Street Academy’s non-traditional program is approved by the NCAA. Beginning with the 2021–22 school year, the courses below carry NCAA approval.

Approved course list

Coursework that counts

Student-athletes can complete the following Cross Street Academy courses with confidence that they are recognized by the NCAA.

  • Algebra 1
  • Geometry
  • Algebra 2
  • Pre-Calculus
  • American History
  • World History
  • Gov’t & Economics
  • English 9, 10, 11, 12
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Spanish 1, 2 and Spanish 2 (Dual Credit)
  • Philosophy

Verify approval

To confirm which courses are approved, visit the NCAA Eligibility Center and search with Cross Street Academy’s NCAA High School Code.
