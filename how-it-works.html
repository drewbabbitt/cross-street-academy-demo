How It Works

A day in the life of a Cross Street Academy student

Similar to a traditional school day — just online. Students meet their teachers in live classes each day, with on-demand recordings and weekly help sessions to support them.

The school day

Three parts to every school day

Sign in, learn live alongside your teachers and classmates, then continue with the online curriculum at your own pace.

1

Prepare for class

A few minutes before your first class, open your PC or laptop and visit CrossStreetAcademy.com. Click the login button to reach your personalized dashboard, where your schedule for the day and a link to each of your assigned live classes are waiting.

2

Participate in live lectures

Shortly before class begins, click the course link to join the shared live classroom. Classes typically last 40 minutes, and you can interact with your teacher and classmates throughout. After a short break, join your next class the same way — with a longer lunch break between classes 3 and 4.

3

Continue with online resources

After your final class — typically early afternoon — your accompanying online curriculum offers lessons, practice sets and quizzes that correspond to the live teaching. Each class also holds a weekly live help session for extra support from your teacher; links and times are posted on your dashboard.

Flexibility built in

Live, and on demand

Every class video is available for on-demand viewing. If your family needs flexibility, or you simply want to revisit a topic from the live class, the entire video library is open to you at any time.

Want to see a sample class video? Contact us and we’ll gladly share one — just note that it can’t capture the live interaction that happens with students.
