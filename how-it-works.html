How It Works

A day in the life of a Cross Street Academy student

Similar to a traditional school day — just online.

1

Prepare for class

Get on your PC or laptop a few minutes before your first class and visit CrossStreetAcademy.com. Click the login button to access your customized dashboard with your schedule for the day and links to each of your assigned live classes.

2

Participate in lectures

A couple of minutes before class starts, click the course link to join the shared live classroom. Classes typically last 40 minutes, and you can interact with the teacher and other students throughout. After a short break, join your next class the same way — with a longer lunch break between classes 3 and 4.

3

Continue with online resources

After your final class (typically early afternoon), your accompanying online curriculum offers lessons, practice sets and quizzes that correspond to the live teaching. Each class also has a weekly live help session for extra support from your teacher — links and times are on your dashboard.

All class videos are available for on-demand viewing. If you need flexibility or want to revisit a topic from the live class, you have access to the entire video library at any time.
