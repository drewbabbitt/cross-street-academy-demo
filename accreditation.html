Accreditation

Accreditation, approvals, membership and dual enrollment

Accreditation

NEASC

Cross Street Academy is an accredited online program through the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC).

Membership

ACSI

CSA is a member of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI).

Approvals

NCAA · College Board · State of Connecticut

CSA's program is approved by the NCAA (eligible courses). Our high school program is approved to offer Advanced Placement (AP) classes — College Board school code 070481. CSA is approved as a non-public school by the State of Connecticut Department of Education.

Dual Enrollment

College at Southeastern

CSA has partnered with the College at Southeastern to offer courses taught by our teachers that earn both high school credits and college credits.
