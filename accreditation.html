Accreditation & Approvals

Accreditation, approvals, membership & dual enrollment

The credentials behind a Cross Street education: regional accreditation through NEASC, approvals from the NCAA, the College Board, and the State of Connecticut, membership in ACSI, and college credit through the College at Southeastern.

Standards & Recognition

Credentials your family can count on

An online school should be able to show its work. Each recognition below is held by an outside institution — so transcripts, coursework, and credits from Cross Street Academy carry weight beyond our own walls.

Accreditation

New England Association of Schools & Colleges

Cross Street Academy is an accredited online program through the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC).

Membership

Association of Christian Schools International

Cross Street Academy is a member of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI).

Approvals

NCAA · College Board · State of Connecticut

Cross Street Academy’s program is approved by the NCAA — see our NCAA-eligible courses. Our high school program is approved to offer Advanced Placement (AP) classes under College Board school code 070481, and CSA is approved as a non-public school by the State of Connecticut Department of Education.

Dual Enrollment

College at Southeastern

Cross Street Academy has partnered with the College at Southeastern to offer courses — taught by our own teachers — that earn high school and college credit at the same time.
