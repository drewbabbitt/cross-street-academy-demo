Our Teachers

World-class educators

Our teachers are hand-picked from across the country — proven subject-matter experts in the courses they teach, and every one of them teaches from a Christian worldview.

Faculty & Staff

Leadership, faculty, and counseling

Mr. Scott Saiers

Head of School

Graduated Phi Beta Kappa from the University of Virginia in Economics with a perfect 800 Math SAT. Twenty years on Wall Street applying statistics, probability and derivatives daily; Managing Director at UBS by 29 running the Convertible Bond desk, then senior executive at a derivative-focused hedge fund. Shows how math is used in everyday life and how the order of numbers points to the Creator.

Mr. Paul Hicok

History / High School Principal

Masters in Christian Apologetics from Biola University and a B.A. in Political Science from UC Davis. Over 18 years in Christian education as administrator (Vice Principal) and teacher. Holds a Professional Lifetime Certification in Christian Apologetics and Social Science from ACSI. Passionate about teaching students, families and educators to embrace and live out the Christian worldview; also teaches Government & Politics, Economics, and Philosophy.

Mrs. Alex Erb

Science

B.A. from Biola University with 10 years in Christian education teaching K-12 STEM and Technology at private Christian schools in Silicon Valley and Denver. Built a new STEM program from the ground up, sourcing curriculum and coordinating across science and math. Completed Digital Media and Technology coursework at Stanford and Fresno Pacific. Shows students how science points to the truths of the Bible.

Mrs. Krista McGee

English

B.A. in English from the University of South Florida and 15 years teaching 7th-12th grade English and Drama (including AP) at private Christian schools. ACSI-certified. Author of nine young adult Christian fiction novels published by HarperCollins and Amaris Media, and has graded thousands of AP English essays for ETS.

Dr. Debbie Romero

Math

Ed.D. in Educational Leadership (Calvary Chapel University), Master of Human Relations (University of Oklahoma), B.S. in Bible and Secondary Education, Mathematics (Calvary University). Over 35 years in Christian education as math teacher, tutor and administrator, including Director of Curriculum, Instruction, and Accreditation. Loves exploring the beauty, order and wonder of mathematics.

Dr. David McGee

Bible

Ed.D. from Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary, Th.M. from Dallas Theological Seminary. ACSI Professional Lifetime Certification in Bible and Mathematics. Almost 20 years teaching Bible and Math at private Christian schools, plus Bible, Theology, and Apologetics at several Christian universities.

Ms. Lisa Grimo

English & History

Studied at the New England Conservatory of Music, earned a bachelor's in liberal studies (Excelsior College) and a master's in English education (Liberty University, 2017). Has taught English in Christian and public schools plus math, history, science, psychology, Bible, world religions, and music. Adjunct faculty at Southeastern University since 2019.

Mrs. Lori White

Science & Math

B.A. in Chemistry Education and M.Ed. from Olivet Nazarene University; Woodrow Wilson Fellow with an MBA in Educational Leadership (University of Indianapolis). Seventeen years teaching math and science, six as assistant principal. Sees math and science as ways to understand God more deeply through His creation.

Mrs. Emma Mitchell

Guidance Counselor

Master's in clinical mental health counseling and bachelor's in psychology from Truett McConnell University. Two years in Christian private practice with children, teens, adults and couples. Helps students navigate struggles, pressures and life changes through a biblical perspective.
