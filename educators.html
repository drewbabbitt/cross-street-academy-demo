For Educators

Have a passion for teaching?

But tired of losing the authority to teach your best? Become a Cross Street teacher.

Why Cross Street?

  • Convenience — work from home; no commuting
  • Freedom to teach your way — we empower skilled teachers to deploy the strategies most effective for student learning, not direct them on how to teach
  • Fair compensation
  • Easy start — all you need is a computer with a reliable internet connection; everything else is provided

Educator application

If you would like to be considered for teacher positions, please apply for a role as a Cross Street Academy educator.
