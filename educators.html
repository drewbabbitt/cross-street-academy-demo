For Educators

Have a passion for teaching?

But tired of losing the authority to teach your best? Bring your classroom home — become a Cross Street teacher.

Teach with us

Why Cross Street?

  • Convenience — teach from home, with no commute
  • Freedom to teach your way — we empower skilled teachers to deploy the strategies most effective for student learning, rather than direct them on how to teach
  • Fair compensation — pay that respects the work of a professional educator
  • Easy start — all you need is a computer with a reliable internet connection; everything else is provided
